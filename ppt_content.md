## SLIDE 2: WHY AGENTIC AI IS THE ANSWER

### Headline
> **"Small businesses need enterprise-grade finance recovery — without the enterprise-grade team."**

### The Problem with Current Solutions

| Current Approach | Why It Fails |
|-----------------|-------------|
| Manual follow-ups (calls, emails) | 14 hrs/week wasted; inconsistent; emotionally draining |
| Accounting software (Tally, Zoho) | Tracks invoices but does NOT collect money — passive, not active |
| Debt collection agencies | Charge 15-50% of recovered amount; damage customer relationships |
| Hiring a finance team | Costs ₹3-5L/month minimum; unaffordable for ₹10L-5Cr businesses |
| Legal action (MSME Samadhaan) | Slow, adversarial — buyers may stop doing business with you |

### Why Agentic AI Changes Everything

1. **Autonomous Decision-Making**: The AI agent doesn't just notify — it decides WHEN to remind, HOW to escalate, WHAT payment plan to offer, and WHETHER to approve a refund. No human in the loop for routine decisions.

2. **24/7 Operation, Zero Fatigue**: Agents work around the clock. No emotional bias. No "I'll follow up tomorrow." Every overdue invoice gets attention within 24 hours.

3. **Learns and Adapts**: Tracks which reminder style works for each customer. Friendly tone via email worked for Customer A? Use it again. SMS with firm tone worked for Customer B? Noted and repeated.

4. **Cost Efficiency**: AI-powered collection systems reduce operational costs by up to 80% while achieving 47% higher recovery rates (Interval AI, 2025). Automation improves live contact rates by 200-300% (Robotalker).

5. **Relationship Preservation**: Unlike debt collectors, AI agents follow merchant-defined policies. Tone, timing, and escalation are all configurable. No harassment, no damaged relationships.

## SLIDE 3: PROJECT IRIS — THE SOLUTION

### Headline
> **"Four autonomous pillars. One unified cash flow brain."**

### The Four Pillars

#### Pillar 1: COLLECTION AGENT 🔵
**Problem it solves**: Money owed to you is not coming in on time
- Tracks every invoice in a real database (amount, due date, customer, line items)
- Sends automated reminders with escalating urgency: Friendly → Firm → Final Notice
- Learns which reminder style works best per customer (adaptive strategy)
- Offers EMI/payment plans to struggling customers based on merchant policies
- Flags high-risk accounts early using AI-computed risk scores
- Every reminder includes a Pine Labs payment link — one tap to pay

#### Pillar 2: DEDUCTION AGENT 🟢
**Problem it solves**: Customer disputes and refunds eat into your revenue
- Customers raise disputes via the portal — no phone calls needed
- AI verifies claims by cross-referencing order details, delivery proof, photos
- Resolves disputes autonomously: full refund, partial refund, replacement, or rejection
- Follows merchant-defined policy rules (e.g., "auto-approve refunds under ₹500")
- Processes refunds via Pine Labs Gateway
- Logs every decision with full reasoning for merchant audit

#### Pillar 3: TREASURY ENGINE 🟡
**Problem it solves**: You don't know where your money is or where it's going
- Tracks every rupee — incoming collections and outgoing refunds
- Maintains running net cash flow balance
- Generates 90-day cash flow predictions
- Alerts when predicted balance goes negative within 30 days
- Timeline view of all money movement

#### Pillar 4: THREAT DETECTOR + OVERALL CASH FLOW 🔴
**Problem it solves**: Financial risks sneak up on you
- Detects high refund-to-collection ratios
- Flags slow collection trends (average days-to-pay increasing)
- Identifies potential fraud (customer refund spikes)
- Spots unusual payment patterns (multiple failures, rapid refunds)
- Severity-rated alerts: Low → Medium → High → Critical
- AI-generated Quick Summary: your business health in under 200 words

### Pine Labs Integration — The Payment Backbone
- Every reminder, every payment plan, every refund flows through Pine Labs
- Payment links embedded in SMS, email, and WhatsApp messages
- UPI, cards, netbanking, wallets, EMI — all payment modes supported
- Real-time payment confirmation callbacks update invoice status within 60 seconds
- Refund processing via Pine Labs Refund API with transaction references
- Architecture designed for trivial swap from mock to production Pine Labs SDK

## SLIDE 4: HOW IT WORKS — Technical Flow

### Headline
> **"From overdue invoice to money in your account — fully autonomous."**

### Flow 1: Autonomous Collection Flow

```
📄 Invoice Created
    ↓
⏰ Due Date Passes → Status: OVERDUE
    ↓
🤖 Collection Agent Activates (within 24 hours)
    ↓
📊 Checks Customer Risk Score + Response Profile
    ↓
📧 Sends Adaptive Reminder (best channel + tone for THIS customer)
    ├── Includes Pine Labs Payment Link (one-tap pay)
    ├── Logs action + reasoning in Action Log
    ↓
⏳ No Response in 7 Days?
    ↓
📧 Escalates: Friendly → Firm → Final Notice
    ↓
�� Still No Payment? Offers EMI/Payment Plan (per merchant policy)
    ├── Generates Pine Labs links for each installment
    ↓
⚠️ Flags as High-Risk Account
    ├── Risk Score updated
    ├── Dashboard alert triggered
    ↓
💰 Customer Pays via Pine Labs Link
    ├── Callback received → Invoice marked PAID
    ├── Transaction recorded in Treasury
    ├── Cash flow predictions updated
    └── Customer response profile updated (learning loop)
```

### Flow 2: Autonomous Dispute Resolution Flow

```
😤 Customer Raises Dispute via Portal
    ↓
🤖 Deduction Agent Creates Dispute Record
    ↓
🔍 AI Verifies Claim
    ├── Cross-references: order details, delivery proof, photos
    ├── Missing info? → Requests from customer
    ↓
📋 Evaluates Against Merchant Policy Rules
    ├── "Auto-approve refunds under ₹500" → AUTO-APPROVED
    ├── Amount > threshold → Full evaluation
    ↓
✅ Resolution Determined
    ├── Full Refund → Pine Labs Refund API → Money returned
    ├── Partial Refund → Pine Labs processes partial amount
    ├── Replacement → Merchant notified
    ├── Rejection → Customer notified with reasoning
    ↓
📝 Everything Logged
    ├── Action Log: decision + reasoning + policy rules applied
    ├── Treasury: outgoing transaction recorded
    ├── Merchant notified with summary
    └── Threat Detector: monitors for refund spikes
```

### Flow 3: Treasury Intelligence Flow

```
💰 All Money Movement Tracked
    ├── Every payment → Incoming transaction
    ├── Every refund → Outgoing transaction
    ↓
📊 Running Net Balance Computed (real-time)
    ↓
🔮 90-Day Cash Flow Predictions Generated (daily)
    ├── Based on: pending invoices, expected payments, anticipated refunds
    ↓
🚨 Threat Detection Engine Runs
    ├── High refund ratio? → ALERT
    ├── Slow collections trend? → ALERT
    ├── Customer fraud pattern? → ALERT
    ├── Unusual payment anomalies? → ALERT
    ↓
📝 AI Quick Summary Generated
    └── "Your collection rate improved 12% this month. 3 high-risk
         accounts need attention. Cash flow positive for next 60 days.
         Recommended: Follow up with Customer X (₹2.3L overdue)."
```
